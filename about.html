About Us

"Dedicated to the original dream of art."

The Canadian and American Drawing Artists Association (CAADAA) is a non-commercial organization of artists in Canada and the United States. Our goal is to encourage young artists to develop their drawing skills. We provide an opportunity for renowned artists and exceptional talents to share their drawings and ideas with others on the web.

All images and artists' names have been posted to this website with full permission from the participating artists.
